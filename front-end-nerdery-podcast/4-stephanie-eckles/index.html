Podcasts All from Front End Nerdery Podcast View 4 - Stephanie Eckles on anchor.fm

In  this episode I spoke with Stephanie Eckles. Stephanie is a Front End  focused developer, mom, creator of Style Stage, 11ty.rocks, SmolCSS.dev  and laboratory mastermind of CSS and a11y. We spoke about all of  Stephanie's great projects, how she got into web development, and how  teaching is one of her favorite things as an instructor at egghead.io!  We also talk about CSS and accessibility a bit!
